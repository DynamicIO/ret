Eligibility Requirements

Academic Status

Currently enrolled undergraduate student at a U.S. college or university.

Citizenship

U.S. citizen, U.S. national, or permanent resident (green card holder).

Academic Performance

Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale and good academic standing at home institution.

Coursework

Completed coursework in mathematics (calculus), physics, computer science, or engineering. Programming experience preferred but not required.

Required Documents

Application Tips

Maximize your chances of selection with these guidelines

Personal Statement

Clearly articulate your research interests, career goals, and motivation for quantum/security research.

Strong Recommendations

Choose recommenders who know your academic work well and can speak to your research potential.

Apply Early

Submit your application well before the deadline to avoid technical issues and show enthusiasm.
